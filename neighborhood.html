About

Suncrest is a neighborhood on the west side of Orem, Utah. It's a prime location for people from all walks of life due to its proximity to several major universities (approximately 1/3 of college students in the state of Utah attend a school within 15 miles of Suncrest), and its proximity to many businesses.

Orem is part of the Provo-Orem Metropolitan area and is also close to Silicon Slopes - one of the fastest growing technology/business regions in America.
